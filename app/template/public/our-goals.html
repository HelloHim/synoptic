Our Goal

We thrive on helping Cambodia have the same recourses as the rest of our world.

We belive rural Cambodia should also have a right to clean water, help with flooding, prediction of environmental changes!

Our Journey

We started this journey by creating technology that will help cambodians to understand when they should expect a flood or drought and allow them to understand when their rivers have water safe for farming, drinking, washing, bathing and more.

Weather

Alerts are provided on the weather page for when their is expected to be a drought or flood dependant on the rainfall and water levels recorded over the past week.

We want to reduce the effect of school, business and house flooding in Pu Ngaol by giving them access to warnings of potential future floods allowing them to prepaire early.

This also helps farmers to understand when they should begin to prepaire their fields of the season and also predict their potential yeald to be able to put in specific measures to ensure they can sustain themself and their families.

We also want to help with safety issues by helping to alert residance when they shouldnt use transport such as bikes as their is an increased likelyhood of a storm which could cause them to get sick or stuck on mud tracks or even potentially injoured. furthermore this reduces overcrowding in hospitals.
